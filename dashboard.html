Commission Rules
Define eligibility criteria and commission tiers. Global rule applies to all staff. Individual rules override global for specific staff members.
How it works:
1. Count eligibility — staff must hit the count % threshold to qualify for any commission
2. MRC tiers — once eligible, MRC achievement determines the commission % slab
3. Commission = MRC achieved × tier %
4. Priority — individual rule overrides global rule for that person & month range

Staff must achieve at least this % of their product count target to be eligible for any commission. If below this threshold, no commission is paid regardless of MRC.
Example: If threshold = 90% and count target = 20, staff must close at least 18 products to qualify.

Commission % is applied to MRC achieved. Higher MRC achievement = higher commission slab.
